KumabyUni

The Global University Project.

In the COVID-19 era, we have realized that socially distant learning is an even more viable option than what tradition dictates. This web app takes us in that direction by democratizing education. Anyone can upload lecture videos from their university to this website (with consent from their professor), and make available for public viewing. This video will automatically get processed by a python script, to quickly yet exhaustively remove all traces of personal identifying information (faces, names, etc.), thus mitigating privacy concerns.

Goober Eats

A Delivery Optimizer.

Goober Eats is a food delivery navigation program that is able to generate the shortest route between locations and give turn by turn navigation directions to get to the destination. Using C++, I implemented A* search as well as a simulated annealing algorithm in order to get an efficient solution to the traveling salesman problem.
